Veggie Stir-Fry

Ingredients:

  • Broccoli
  • Carrots
  • Bell Peppers
  • Tofu
  • Soy Sauce

Instructions:

  1. Chop veggies and tofu.
  2. Stir-fry in a pan with soy sauce.
  3. Serve hot.

Quinoa Salad

Ingredients:

  • Cooked Quinoa
  • Cucumbers
  • Tomatoes
  • Red Onion
  • Olive Oil
  • Lemon Juice

Instructions:

  1. Combine all ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Mix well.
  3. Chill before serving.
